Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how this application ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use it.

1. Overview

The App is an open-source Sudoku game for Android. The App itself does not require you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name or email address.

However, the App may use third-party services that automatically collect certain information, primarily for advertising and app analytics.

2. Information Collected

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, the following information may be collected automatically by third-party services:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)

  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)

This information is used to:

  • Display advertisements
